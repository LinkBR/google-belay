About Belay

Belay is a project at Google to investigate and develop new approaches of cloud authorization. The project is currently exploring ways for users to manager their relationships with web sites, and how users might grant one site access to another.

Your Station

In Belay, a "station" is an account with a provider you trust that will hold on to, and manage your relationships.

▸ Advanced Administration

The link to your station is stored in local storage in this browser. These options let you manage that storage, and work with other station providers

Stored Station

This value is the Bcap of your station. Saving and restoring it on another browser will let you access it from elsewhere. Treat it like a password.
